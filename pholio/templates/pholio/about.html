What is Pholio ?

Pholio is a portfolio website but not a traditional one. At pholio, users can register for an account and set up a personal portfolio webpage by just entering their information, without writing any code.

Users have complete control over what sections they want the world to see, what information they want to add in those sections, and all of the information is presented in a beautiful design.
